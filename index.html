About

I'm a researcher and educator working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and music. My work explores how emerging technologies are reshaping the ways we create, experience, and understand sound.

My research combines technical experimentation with critical inquiry, focusing on co-creativity between humans and AI systems.

As a lecturer, I teach machine learning through both theoretical foundations and hands-on creative applications.

Research interests

  • Human–AI co-creativity
  • Generative music systems (transformers, diffusion models)
  • Interactive machine learning
  • Audio representation learning
  • Aesthetics of computational creativity
